About Our School

Faith-based education shaped by care, character, and excellence.

Atmosphere International School is a private educational institution located in Ughelli, Delta State, Nigeria. Established in 2019 (BN-2749989), it operates as a registered partnership under Nigerian education regulations and provides a faith-based, academically focused learning environment for children in early childhood and basic education levels.

The school emphasizes a balance between academic excellence, moral values, and spiritual development. It is known for small class sizes, supportive teachers, and a disciplined, nurturing environment that promotes holistic child development.

Educational Focus

Early childhood, primary, and junior secondary education built on academic excellence, moral values, and ethical development.
